PSFC Shift Monitor SMS Notifications

Program Description

PSFC Shift Monitor sends SMS notifications about available volunteer shifts at the Park Slope Food Coop to opted-in recipients.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. Notifications are sent only when new shifts matching your preferences become available. Expect up to a few messages per day.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your wireless carrier for details.

Opt-Out

Text STOP to unsubscribe from notifications at any time. You will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Help

Text HELP for support information, or contact the account owner directly.

Contact

For questions, contact the account owner directly.
