Privacy Policy

PSFC Shift Monitor

This is a personal, non-commercial application that sends SMS notifications about open volunteer shifts at the Park Slope Food Coop.

Data Collected

We collect only phone numbers of recipients who have provided explicit consent to receive shift notifications.

How Data Is Used

Phone numbers are used solely to send SMS notifications about available shifts. No data is shared with third parties, used for marketing, or sold.

Data Storage

Phone numbers are stored as environment variables in a private GitHub repository and are not publicly accessible.

Opt-Out

Recipients may opt out at any time by contacting the account owner directly. Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the account owner directly.
